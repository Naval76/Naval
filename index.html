About Me

Hi, I'm a passionate graphic designer currently working at Orangutan Gaming, where I specialize in crafting match-day graphics, posters, and flyers for the BGMI and Valorant rosters. My journey with Orangutan Gaming has been incredibly rewarding, as I’ve had the chance to learn from industry experts during bootcamps and collaborate on creative projects that blend my love for esports and design. I’m also a second-year Communication Design student at Atlas SkillTech University, Mumbai, where I’m honing my skills in design thinking and creative storytelling. Balancing my academics with professional work has fueled my growth as a designer, pushing me to innovate and create impactful visuals. With a strong dedication to sports, creativity, and storytelling, I aim to bring vibrant ideas to life through my designs. Welcome to my world of creativity!
